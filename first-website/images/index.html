J.R.R. Tolkien

J.R.R. Tolkien is a famous fantasy author and academic writer.

Tolkien's most famous fictional works include:

  • The Lord of the Rings
  • The Hobbit
  • The Silmarillion

These works take place in a magical universe called "Ea," and within that are the lands Arda and Middle Earth, the latter of which is more commonly known.
